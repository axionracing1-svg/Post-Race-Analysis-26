What penalties cost — points and positions
Enterprise −20pts
The largest single penalty. Likely causes: late digital submission (−5/day), missing printed copies (−5 each), or mismatch between digital and printed versions. This is entirely preventable with a submission checklist.
Engineering −15pts
Possible causes: virtual cargo not marked in drawings (−6pts), missing wing identification (−4pts), surface finish defects (−2pts each), or portfolio mismatch. Cross-check every drawing against the scorecard KPIs.
Racing −4pts
Likely a minor scrutineering deduction — possibly a small dimensional infringement (−4pts standard). Given the 2025 results showed all regulations passed, this suggests a change in the 2026 design introduced a borderline measurement. Worth investigating.
